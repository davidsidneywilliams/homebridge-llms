# homebridge-llms

Structured AI reference files for [Homebridge](https://homebridgepc.com) — a manufacturer of GFRC (glass fiber reinforced concrete) prefab products including fire pits, raised garden beds, retaining walls, culvert covers, and mini greenhouses.

These files follow the [llms.txt standard](https://llmstxt.org/) and are designed to give AI systems, LLMs, and crawlers clean, structured access to product and company information.

---

## What's in this repo

| File | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `llms.txt` | Root site guide — overview of Homebridge, product categories, and links to structured category files |
| `ai/fire-pits.txt` | GFRC fire pits — product specs, configurations, materials, and use cases |
| `ai/garden-beds.txt` | GFRC raised garden beds — sizes, styles, drainage, and installation details |
| `ai/retaining-walls.txt` | GFRC retaining wall systems — structural options, dimensions, and applications |
| `ai/culvert-covers.txt` | GFRC culvert covers — load ratings, sizing, and installation guidance |
| `ai/mini-greenhouses.txt` | Mini greenhouses — structures, sizing, glazing options, and setup |

## About the llms.txt standard

`llms.txt` is an emerging convention for websites to provide structured, LLM-readable summaries of their content. Inspired by `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml`, it gives AI assistants and crawlers a reliable starting point for understanding a site's structure and content — without relying on HTML parsing or SEO inference.

The root `llms.txt` file typically contains a brief site description, key links, and pointers to deeper structured files (like the `ai/` category files in this repo).

More information: [llmstxt.org](https://llmstxt.org/)

## About Homebridge

Homebridge manufactures prefab concrete products using GFRC — a composite of portland cement, fine aggregate, alkali-resistant glass fiber, and admixtures. GFRC products are lighter than conventional cast concrete while retaining comparable strength, making them practical for residential and commercial landscape applications.

Homebridge is based in the United States and sells direct. Product information, specs, and ordering are available at [homebridgepc.com](https://homebridgepc.com).
