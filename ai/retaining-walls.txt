## What Is GFRC?

GFRC stands for Glass Fiber Reinforced Concrete — a cement-based matrix reinforced with alkali-resistant glass fibers. The result is a material that is three to four times stronger than standard concrete while remaining lightweight enough for efficient installation without machinery.

- **12,500 psi compressive strength** — structural integrity under load and ground movement
- **Freeze-thaw immunity** — no cracking or spalling in cold climates
- **50+ year expected lifespan** — engineered for permanence, not seasonal replacement
- **Zero maintenance** — no sealing, painting, or surface treatment required
